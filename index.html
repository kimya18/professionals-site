About

Sakrei Psychological Services provides comprehensive evaluations for ADD; ADHD; various learning disabilities; and executive, behavioral, and emotional functioning.

Dr. Sakhai Kreinik has over 20 years of experience in conducting in-depth cognitive assesments for these conditions. She earned her Ph.D. in Clinical and School Psychology from Hofstra University. Dr. Sakhai Kreinik has extensive expertise in uncovering factors that hinder learning and retention.
